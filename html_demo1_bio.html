Andy was born many years ago in Altoona, PA. He joined the Army after high school and got to visit Fort Jackson, Fort Gordon, Fort Sill and Turkey.

After the Army, Andy worked many jobs including as an estimator for a construction subcontractor, a ski instructor, and a math tutor.

While working a job in 2005, Andy suffered a spinal cord injury (level T-11) and is now a paralysed from the waist down. He uses a wheelchair to get around, and hand controls to drive.

After the injury, Andy started school toward the goal of finding a new career. He completed a bachelors in psychology, started toward a master's in counselling, but stopped and decided on a certificate from NCSU in computer science.

Andy was hired by the Tolliver Group as a software engineer and started working as part of the Advanced Distributive Learning (ADL) Initiative on May 1, 2015.

Andy is married to the lovely Susan and they have a cat and a dog, Fluffbutton and Remy, respectively.
